FAQ

I need to download the app after a new release?

Absolutely... Not ! The desktop app checks the updates and update itself if needed. After an update, a changelog will be displayed.

Does the app still receive updates?

Yes, it does, albeit less often as we have less time to work on it. However, when we find anything that needs improvement, encounter bugs, or receive feedback, we try to address it as quickly as possible.

Has the Android app development started?

Not yet, as we currently don't have the time or immediate need for it. However, it is still on our minds.

An internet connection is required?

Yes in order to sync your data between multiple computers or just using the incoming android app, an internet connection is required to save your data.

How can I submit a bug I've encountered?

For now, you can join our Discord (the link is at the bottom of the page), where there is a bug report section. We are also considering adding either a contact form or an email address to this website.

I don't remember my password!

That's not a question, but we've got you covered. There is a feature to create a new password on the login screen.

I've got a problem, where can I find some logs ?

Our logs are in MyDocuments/MyVGT/LOGS/log.txt. If you want or need to, don't hesitate to sent it to us !

How's the profitability system is working ?

We are taking your playtime into account for that one, and your lists too. Let's get an example : You've set the profitability multiplier to 2, so you have to play 2 time the price paid to have a game flag as profitable. What features are you planning?

  • More stats on the home page
  • More detailed error messages (including consequences, workarounds, etc.)
  • Allowing you to search for a game by its name in your lists
  • Improving the app to make it faster, more robust, and more beautiful
  • The Android app
  • Retrospective on the past years. Considering how we implemented it, we strongly recommend not to switch to different accounts on the desktop app, it would remove your progress as we intend to make it save locally only (yet).
